The problem

Good local businesses lose enquiries because their website makes things hard.

Customers want quick answers: what you do, where you work, whether you can help, and how to contact you. If that is unclear, they move on.

Hard to use on mobile

Most customers check from their phone. Slow pages, tiny text, hidden phone numbers, and confusing menus cost real enquiries.

Not clear what you offer

Products, services, locations, delivery areas, photos, pricing guidance, and quote options need to be easy to find.

Weak local visibility

Your site and Google profile should help locals find you for the jobs, towns, and services you actually want.
