Overview

Bush Digital Guides (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website bushdigitalguides.com.au and associated internal business tools. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

Information We Collect

Website Visitors

  • Standard analytics data (page views, traffic sources) via Google Analytics
  • No personal information is collected from website visitors unless voluntarily provided (e.g., via email contact)

Google Workspace Integration

We use an internal Google Workspace application to manage business operations including:

  • Gmail: Reading and sending business emails
  • Google Calendar: Managing appointments and schedules
  • Google Drive: Storing and organising business documents
  • Google Contacts: Managing business contacts
  • Google Sheets & Docs: Creating and editing business documents

This application is used solely for internal business administration by authorised personnel. It does not access, collect, or process data belonging to third parties beyond what is necessary for standard business communication.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to customer enquiries
  • To manage appointments and job scheduling
  • To organise internal business documents
  • To analyse website traffic and improve our content

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Google Workspace data remains within Google’s infrastructure and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy
  • Access to business tools is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties

Data Retention

  • Business communications are retained in accordance with Australian tax and business record-keeping requirements (generally 5 years)
  • Website analytics data is retained for up to 14 months

Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Make a complaint about how we handle your information
