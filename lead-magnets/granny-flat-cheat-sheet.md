## The Gotchas That Kill Profits

### 1. Drainage & Stormwater
Rear-sloping sites can add $10,000â€“$30,000 in drainage works. Check the Deposited Plan for easements before committing.

### 2. Sewer
Building near sewer mains triggers zone-of-influence assessment. Building OVER sewer mains requires encasement â€” very costly. Get a sewer diagram ($26 from Sydney Water or equivalent).

### 3. Bushfire (BAL Levels)
- BAL-Low to BAL-29: Manageable (adds $0â€“$15k)
- BAL-40 or Flame Zone: Can double build costs. May make project unfeasible.

### 4. Easements
Building over easements is generally prohibited. This can make a granny flat impossible â€” check the DP before purchasing.

### 5. Infrastructure Charges
- **NSW:** $20,000â€“$30,000+ in growth areas (s7.11 contributions + HPC)
- **QLD:** ~$32,000 per dwelling (some council discounts available)
- **SA:** ~$6,000 per dwelling

### 6. Hidden Costs in Builder Quotes
Things often NOT included in the "turnkey" price:
- [ ] Air conditioning
- [ ] Fly screens
- [ ] NBN connection
- [ ] Smoke alarms
- [ ] Landscaping and turf
- [ ] Fencing between dwellings

**Always get a written list of inclusions and exclusions.**

### 7. Valuation Risk
If nobody in the suburb is paying $1.1M, you won't get a $1.1M valuation â€” no matter how nice your granny flat is.
