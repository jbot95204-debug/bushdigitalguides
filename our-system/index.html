Approval gates

Nothing important should happen silently.

For Bush Digital Guides, the current operating rule is simple: prepare the asset, check it privately, then approve before anything is sent, published, changed, or connected to a live external system.

That same approach is how we build client systems. Drafts, profile updates, review requests, customer replies, quote follow-ups, and assistant workflows should have a clear human approval point unless the owner has explicitly approved automation.

As AI-capable tools make scams, impersonation, scraping, spam, and probing easier, our care work should keep websites, forms, email/domain setup, access, backups, and assistant guardrails current over time.
