Approval guardrails

Useful support, with the owner still in control.

Some businesses need simple reminders and tracking. Others may benefit from approved support for FAQs, missed calls, draft replies, or admin summaries where it genuinely saves time. The rule is the same: the owner stays in control, and the system only acts inside approved boundaries.

Rules

Approved wording only

Customer-facing wording, FAQs, review prompts, and follow-up messages use agreed information. No invented prices, unapproved promises, or advice outside the approved business wording.

Draft

Drafts before sends

Reply drafts, review request drafts, quote follow-up reminders, and weekly admin summaries are prepared for review. Customer sends, bookings, quoting, legal, compliance, or sensitive trade decisions stay behind approval gates.

Hand

Safe handoff

When a customer needs pricing, availability, a firm booking, technical advice, or a complaint handled properly, the system captures the context and passes it to the owner or team.

Risk

Safety checks included

Setups include practical guardrails: no passwords, card or bank details, private customer lists, unapproved promises, or risky actions without owner-approved rules and a clear handoff path.

Missed-call safety net

Show the simple fallback before adding more process.

A good first setup does not replace the owner. The normal business number rings first. If the call is missed, busy, unavailable, or after-hours, the fallback takes the message, asks only useful questions, and sends a clear owner summary for human follow-up.
