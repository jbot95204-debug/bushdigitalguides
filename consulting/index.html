A higher-value internal workflow assistant for local businesses that need steadier admin support. AI Phone & Chat Assistants

Practical voice and chat support for missed calls and after-hours enquiries. Used carefully, an AI voice call bot can act as the intake layer for the enquiry or admin assistant system by collecting details, answering approved basics, creating lead notes, and handing off when approval or judgement is needed.

Safe

Practical Digital Safety

Website Safety & Growth Checks cover SSL, forms, email/domain records, access, backups, and assistant guardrails. As AI tools make scams and automated abuse easier, we help keep useful systems current without fear-based cyber jargon.

Assistant guardrails

Useful support, with the owner still in control.

These assistant systems are built for practical admin support, not replacing staff or making judgement calls. They work from approved information, keep records clear, and use human approval where the risk is higher.

FAQs

Approved answers only

Assistants answer agreed FAQs, collect contact details, and ask the next useful question. They do not invent prices, give trade advice, or make promises outside the approved business wording.

Draft

Drafts before sends

Reply drafts, review request drafts, quote follow-up reminders, and weekly admin summaries are prepared for review. Customer sends, bookings, quoting, legal, compliance, or sensitive trade decisions stay behind approval gates.

Hand

Safe handoff

When a customer needs pricing, availability, a firm booking, technical advice, or a complaint handled properly, the system captures the context and passes it to the owner or team.

Risk

Safety checks included

Assistant setups include practical guardrails: no passwords, card or bank details, prompt leakage, unapproved promises, or risky actions without owner-approved rules and a clear handoff path.
