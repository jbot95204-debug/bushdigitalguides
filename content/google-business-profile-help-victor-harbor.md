Your Google Business Profile is often the first thing a local customer sees. If it is incomplete, inconsistent, or out of date, people may never reach your website.

Bush Digital Guides helps regional trade and service businesses improve the practical parts of their Google presence without changing anything without approval.

## What a good profile should make clear

A useful Google Business Profile should show:

- the real business name customers know
- accurate categories and services
- current opening hours
- service areas you genuinely cover
- a working phone and website link
- recent project or team photos
- reviews that reflect current work
- responses that sound like the business

## Common issues

Many regional businesses have a profile that was set up years ago and then left alone. Common gaps include old photos, vague service categories, missing services, no review request process, and a website link that sends customers to an unclear page.
