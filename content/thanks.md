Thanks for getting in touch with Bush Digital Guides.

If you emailed about a Free Local Growth Review, we usually reply within 1 business day. The first reply will either confirm the details we need or give you a clear timeframe for the review.

## What happens next

1. We check the public-facing basics: your website, Google Profile, reviews, enquiry path, and follow-up signals.
2. We look at the business from a local customer’s point of view.
3. You receive a short, practical summary with the clearest priority fixes.

Nothing is changed, sent, published, or contacted without your approval.
