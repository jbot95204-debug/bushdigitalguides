A website review should do more than comment on design. For a Victor Harbor trade or service business, the important question is whether the website helps local customers understand, trust, contact, and follow up with you.

Bush Digital Guides reviews the website as part of the wider local growth system.

## What we check

The review looks at:

- the first screen on mobile
- service clarity
- local wording and service areas
- calls, quote buttons, forms, and email paths
- trust signals such as reviews, photos, licences, and experience
- page titles and search basics
- whether the next step is obvious
- what happens after an enquiry is made

## Why this matters

Many local websites look fine but still leak enquiries. The phone number might be hard to tap, the quote form might ask too much or too little, reviews might be hidden, or the service pages might be too vague for a customer comparing options.
