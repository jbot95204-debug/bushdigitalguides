    <div class="bdg-section-heading">
      <div class="section-label">How we deliver</div>
      <h2>A controlled path from first conversation to a working system.</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="bdg-process-line">
      <div><span>01</span><h3>Qualify</h3><p>Confirm the business, bottleneck, budget, enquiry volume and likely value of solving the problem.</p></div>
      <div><span>02</span><h3>Baseline</h3><p>Record the current response time, missed enquiries, follow-up coverage, conversion or administrative effort relevant to the project.</p></div>
      <div><span>03</span><h3>Map</h3><p>Follow the real journey from customer interest through to booking, quoting, delivery and follow-up.</p></div>
      <div><span>04</span><h3>Prioritise</h3><p>Choose one expensive or frustrating bottleneck rather than attempting to automate everything.</p></div>
      <div><span>05</span><h3>Design</h3><p>Give the proposed workflow a clear trigger, owner, approved action, exception path, success measure and manual fallback.</p></div>
      <div><span>06</span><h3>Build</h3><p>Use a controlled test environment and synthetic data before introducing customer information.</p></div>
      <div><span>07</span><h3>Pilot</h3><p>Begin with one service, channel, team member or after-hours use case.</p></div>
      <div><span>08</span><h3>Verify</h3><p>Test normal situations, incorrect information, unusual requests, uncertainty and human escalation.</p></div>
      <div><span>09</span><h3>Launch</h3><p>Go live with monitoring, failure alerts and a quick-disable process.</p></div>
      <div><span>10</span><h3>Improve</h3><p>Compare performance at 30, 60 and 90 days before expanding the system.</p></div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="bdg-section bdg-muted-section">
  <div class="container tech-layout">
    <div>
      <div class="section-label">Implementation principles</div>
      <h2>Responsible systems, not unbounded automation.</h2>
      <p>We use reliable software, automation and carefully configured AI only where they improve response times, customer service or how work moves through the business.</p>
      <ul class="service-area-list">
        <li>Deterministic software handles critical rules and repeatable actions.</li>
        <li>AI may help interpret, classify, summarise or prepare drafts.</li>
        <li>People remain responsible where judgement, pricing or commitments matter.</li>
        <li>Every live workflow has a human handoff, failure alert and manual fallback.</li>
        <li>Core accounts, domains and business data remain owned by the client.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
